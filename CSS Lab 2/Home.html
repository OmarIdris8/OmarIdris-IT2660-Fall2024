Try something new and have fun!

At FuzzyWall Art Studio, we give you the tools you need to learn different mediums of art, including a recording studio! We opened this year and are excited to support our city. All of our instructors formed this studio together with the vision of giving artists, who can't access the supplies they need, all the tools to try everything. Nothings holding you back, so let's get creative.

We are open everyday of the week with regular events and classes for everyone.
